### 1. Project Title
**PolicyPal: AI-Powered HR Handbook & Employee Assistant**

### 2. Real-World Context / Business Background
In medium-to-large companies, HR departments are overwhelmed with repetitive questions from employees: *"How many sick days do I get?", "Is dental covered in my insurance?", "What is the procedure for remote work?"*. These answers exist in the **Employee Handbook**, but nobody reads the 50-page PDF. Employees waste time searching, and HR managers waste time answering emails. Companies need a system that makes policy information instantly accessible and easy to understand.

### 3. Problem Statement
Develop **PolicyPal**, an intelligent HR portal. The system allows HR Managers to upload policy documents (PDFs/Docs). The core feature is a **RAG-based Chatbot** that allows employees to ask questions in natural language and receive accurate answers cited directly from the company policy. Additionally, the system should assist HR in **Drafting New Policies** and **Generating Compliance Quizzes** to ensure employees have actually understood the rules.

### 4. Target Users
- **Employees:** Who need quick answers without emailing HR.
- **HR Managers:** Who manage documentation and need help drafting formal announcements.
- **New Hires:** Who need to get up to speed with company culture quickly.

### 5. Core AI Features
The project must include the following 5 AI-driven features:

1.  **Semantic Policy Search (The "Locator")**
    *   Employees can search using natural language: *"Can I work from Goa?"*
    *   The system uses **Pinecone** to find the specific section in the "Remote Work Policy" document that matches this intent, even if the text uses formal words like "Telecommuting" or "Work from Anywhere."
2.  **Context-Aware HR Chatbot (RAG)**
    *   The employee asks: *"What is the maternity leave entitlement?"*
    *   The AI retrieves the "Leave Policy" section and generates a polite, concise answer: *"According to Section 4.2, you are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. You must apply 8 weeks in advance."*
3.  **Policy Draft Generator (Content Generation)**
    *   HR needs to create a new rule. They input: *"Draft a policy for using AI tools like ChatGPT at work. Strict data privacy, but allowed for coding."*
    *   The AI generates a formal, legally-sound policy document titled **"Generative AI Usage Guidelines,"** ready to be published.
4.  **Compliance Quiz Generator**
    *   After a new policy is uploaded (e.g., "Data Security"), the AI analyzes the text and automatically generates a **5-question MCQ Quiz**.
    *   Employees must pass this quiz to prove they read the policy.
5.  **Anonymous Feedback Summarizer**
    *   Employees can submit anonymous complaints or feedback about company culture.
    *   The AI analyzes these inputs to detect **Sentiment Trends** (e.g., "70% of feedback regarding the new cafeteria food is Negative") and summarizes the key grievances for HR.

### 6. High-Level System Architecture

*   **Frontend (React):** An Employee Dashboard (Chat interface + Document Library) and an HR Admin Dashboard (Uploads + Analytics).
*   **Backend (Node.js + Express):** APIs for file handling, chat logic, and quiz generation.
*   **Database (MongoDB Atlas):**
    *   Stores: User Profiles, Policy Metadata (Title, Date), Quiz Scores, and Feedback Logs.
*   **Vector Database (Pinecone):**
    *   Stores: Embeddings of the **Policy Document Chunks**. This is the "Brain" of the chatbot.
*   **AI Engine (Google AI Studio - Gemini):**
    *   Used for: RAG responses, Policy Drafting, Quiz Generation, and Sentiment Analysis.

### 7. AI & RAG Workflow Explanation

**Phase A: Knowledge Base Ingestion**
1.  **Upload:** HR uploads `Employee_Handbook_2025.pdf`.
2.  **Chunking:** Node.js splits the PDF into logical sections (e.g., "Leave", "Harassment", "IT Security").
3.  **Embedding:** Gemini creates vectors for each section.
4.  **Storage:** Pinecone stores vectors with metadata `{ category: "Leave", policyId: "101" }`.

**Phase B: The Employee Chat (RAG Flow)**
1.  **User Question:** *"Does the company pay for my gym membership?"*
2.  **Retrieval:** System embeds the question and queries Pinecone.
3.  **Result:** It finds a chunk from "Wellness Benefits" that mentions "Fitness Reimbursement".
4.  **Augmentation:**
    *   *Context:* (Retrieved text: "Employees are eligible for up to $50/month reimbursement for fitness centers...").
    *   *Question:* "Does the company pay for gym?"
5.  **Generation:** Gemini answers: *"Yes, the company offers a reimbursement of up to $50/month for fitness centers under the Wellness Benefits policy."*

### 8. Expected Learning Outcomes
*   **Internal Search:** Building a search engine for "Private Data" (unlike Google which searches the public web).
*   **Role-Based Access:** Understanding that HR (Admin) and Employees (Users) see different views in the app.
*   **Content Generation:** Using AI to write formal business documents (Policy Drafting).
*   **Compliance Logic:** Combining AI (Quiz Generation) with Database logic (Saving scores to verify compliance).

### 9. Constraints & Assumptions
*   **File Handling:** Focus on text/PDF files. No need to handle image-based scanned documents.
*   **Privacy:** Since this is a student project, use a "Dummy Handbook" (generated by AI) rather than real sensitive company data.
*   **User Auth:** Simple Login (HR vs Employee) to show different dashboards.

### 10. Optional Extensions (For Advanced Students)
*   **Slack/Teams Bot Integration:** Instead of a web dashboard, allow users to chat with the bot via a Slack webhook.
*   **Multi-Language Policies:** Allow employees to view the English policy translated into their native language (e.g., Hindi/Spanish) in real-time.
*   **Conflict Detector:** When HR drafts a new policy, the AI checks if it contradicts an *existing* policy in the database (e.g., "New rule says 9-5 work, but Old rule says Flexible hours").
